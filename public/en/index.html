Hungary: the gateway between East and West

Hungary’s geography and development policy position the country as a trade bridge connecting East and West. The EU aims to shift at least 30% of 300km+ road freight to rail/water by 2030, driving a rail-logistics investment wave.

The Budapest–Belgrade railway, the East-West Gate intermodal terminal, and large-scale industrial projects in Debrecen elevate Hungary’s logistics infrastructure.

Our portfolio offers rail-connected and motorway-adjacent sites for transshipment, value-add and EU distribution along the East–West corridor.
